WHO ARE WE?

At Off-Grid we design and rent tiny cabins. We started Off-Grid after struggling to find a place to stay near almost every national park. It was often too cold to camp and hotels were hours away. So, we built a better way to enjoy nature.

HOW ARE WE DIFFERENT?

Off-Grid provides the secluded experience of camping while providing all the amenities you'd expect at home. Our 50 square foot window walls ensure that you are both comfortable and completely immersed in nature.
